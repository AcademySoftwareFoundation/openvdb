= OpenVDB Sample Points =

"""Samples grid values of a VDB onto points."""

The OpenVDB Sample Points SOP samples OpenVDB grid values as attributes on spatially located particles. Currently the grid values can be scalar (float, double) or vec3 (float, double) but the attributes on the particles are single precision scalar or vec3.

@parameters

Group:
    #id: group

    Which of the input VDBs to process.

Verbose:
    Prints the sequence of operations to the terminal.
