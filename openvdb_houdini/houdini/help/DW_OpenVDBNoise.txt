= OpenVDB Noise =

"""Applies noise to level sets represented by VDBs."""

The OpenVDB Noise SOP can apply noise to level sets represented by VDBs which can
optionally be masked by another level set. This uses a Fractal Boltzman Generator
for the noise.

@parameters

Group:
    #id: group

    Which of the input VDBs to process.

Amplitude:
    Amplitude of the noise.

Frequency:
    Frequency of the noise.

Octaves:
    Number of octaves for the noise.

Lacunarity:
    Lacunarity of the noise.

Gain:
    Gain of the noise.

Roughness:
    Roughness of the noise.

Surface Offset:
    Offset from the Isosurface of the Level Set on which to apply the noise.

Noise Offset:
    Offset of the noise in 3D world-space.

Noise Mode:
    Straight, Absolute or Inverse Absolute modes.
