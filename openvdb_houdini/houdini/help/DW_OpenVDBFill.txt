= OpenVDB Fill =

"""Fills and activates/deactivates specific regions of voxels within a VDB volume primitive."""

[Include:volume_types]

@parameters

Group:
    The name(s) of the VDB primitives to fill.
    See [specifying volumes|/model/volumes#group].

Bounds:
    The bounds method to use.

    Index Space:
    Interpret the given min and max coordinates in index-space units.

    World Space:
    Interpret the given min and max coordinates in world-space units.

    Reference Geometry:
    Use the world-space bounds of the reference input geometry.

Min Coord:
    The minimum voxel-space or world-space co-ordinate.

Max Coord:
    The maximum voxel-space or world-space co-ordinate.

Value:
    The value with which to fill voxels (y and z are ignored when filling scalar grids).

Active:
    When enabled, activate voxels in the fill region, otherwise deactivate them.
